Help

Need assistance? We’re here to help you make the most of Girlstrual. Below are some common questions and resources to get you started:
1. How do I track my menstrual cycle?
Simply go to the calendar and add your menstruation dates. The app will automatically track your cycle and predict future dates.
2. How does the mood tracker work?
Log your daily mood in the Mood Tracker. Over time, you’ll see patterns that help you understand how your mood relates to your cycle.
3. How can I post or comment on the social platform?
Join the Girlstrual community by creating a profile. Once logged in, you can post your tips, share experiences, and comment on others’ posts to support fellow women.
4. Where can I find my saved notes?
Access your personal notes by navigating to the Notes section from your profile. Here, you can add, edit, or delete any notes you’ve made.
5. How do I reset my password?
If you’ve forgotten your password, click on ‘Forgot Password’ on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.
Still have questions?
If you need further assistance, don’t hesitate to reach out to our support team at support@girlstrual.com. We’re here to help!
