Menstrual Cycle Tracker

Track your menstrual cycle and understand the changes in your body.

Luteal Phase (Days 15-28):

Pre-menstrual phase (Days 20-28): Duration: 7 days

Progesterone levels increase

Uterine lining prepares for potential pregnancy

Breast tenderness, mood swings, cramping

Common Issues:

  • Mood swings
  • Bloating
  • Cravings

Menstrual Phase (Days 1-5)

Duration: 5 days

Hormone levels drop

Common Issues:

  • Fatigue
  • Cramps
  • Mood changes

Follicular Phase (Days 6-14)

Duration: 9 days

Ovulation (Days 10-16, typically 14 days before next menstruation)

Estrogen levels increase

Uterine lining regeneration

Cervical mucus increases

Common Issues:

  • Increased energy
  • Better mood
  • PMS symptoms

Understanding your cycle can help you manage symptoms better and improve your overall health.
